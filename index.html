ABOUT US

Back in 1996, when our grocer delivered 5 kilos of unrequired Maida (all purpose flour) to us, it was quite obvious and known policy at our place by my grandmaa that once food entered in our place cannot go back to store, We all freaked out and didn't know how to use it and certainly not to waste it. My maa and badi maa decided to get creative and made some snacks for the family. Little did they know that this is going to be our dependency for next several decades. Five kilograms and 4 dishes they made, Shakkarpara, Mathri, Namakpara, and Saloni. Everybody absolutely loved it and couldn't get over from the taste. We manufactured and distributed from our place only, Till 2005 when we first rented a small place to conduct our business. In 2015 fall we bought a shop, tried turning that place into a comfort zone for all of us. What started from kitchen is now a leading brand in wholesale market where we manufacture 150+ delicacies with more than 220 varieties including various gujrati farsan available with us. We can assure you that all our products are made with high quality of raw material with all the necessary safety measures and precautions. We at SHREEJI are delighted to serve you
